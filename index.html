Tips from a Wilderness Explorer

Hello there. Here are some tips coming from a Wilderness Explorer about what you should bring during your journey, and some places to visit as well.

The content you will see here will be similar to the greatest YouTube channel of all time, the Outdoor Boys.

America is a very big place.

Alaska, the largest state in the US, is home to some amazing nature and wilderness unlike any other state. Also the perfect spot to challenge oneself into becoming a true Wilderness Explorer, just like the Outdoor Boys.
But if you are a beginner at this kind of experience, I would suggest something a bit easier and not as punishing.

Not as dangerous and punishing, but still offers immaculate views, camping in the Grand Tetons National Park is a great option.
Even though it is free, due to the popularity, reservations usually happen at least a year beforehand.

Number 1 Tip to Always Keep in Mind:

If you are going hiking in the woods or mountains, ALWAYS bring Bear Spray with you at all times.
